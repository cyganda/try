The Philippines (Filipino: Pilipinas or Filipinas) , officially the Republic of the Philippines (Filipino: Republika ng Pilipinas), is an archipelagic country in Southeast Asia. Situated in the western Pacific Ocean, it consists of about 7,641 islands that are broadly categorized under three main geographical divisions from north to south: Luzon, Visayas, Mindanao.

NATIONAL FOOD

Lechon is also considered the national dish of the Philippines. The city of Cebu is considered one of the most famous places in the country for lechon. It is a dish that is almost a fixed part and menu centerpiece of any Filipino celebration which is usually prepared for fiestas, family celebrations (like parties and marriages) and also a favorite dish during Christmas.
